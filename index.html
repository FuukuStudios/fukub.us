F♡ckubus is a satirical action RPG about Miko, our asexual protagonist, who finds themself in the fiery depths of the underworld. The only chance of escaping is by partnering with Mari, a bitchy and feisty succubus who harbors a disdain for everyone – especially Miko. Together, they'll put their hatred for eachother aside, navigating, adventuring, and DEFEATING the infamous 9 circles of hell. Unfortunately, it is easier said than done. This quest for repentance will force Miko and Mari to encounter even bitchier succubi - aka succubitches - and other things that you can only find out if you play the game. No spoilers.

The pages and information regarding F♡ckubus will describe Miko as if he is male. I currently plan to allow choice of gender in the full game.
